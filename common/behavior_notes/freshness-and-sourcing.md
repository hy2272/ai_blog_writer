# Freshness & sourcing for a fast-moving AI story

**When this applies:** research (S1) and any time the audit WARNs on source age.

**The pattern / rule:** for an AI hot topic, prefer the primary source published
closest to the event, and date-anchor every time-sensitive claim so it ages gracefully.

**Before → after:**
- ❌ cite a secondary roundup blog that paraphrases the lab's announcement.
- ✅ cite the lab's own release/post (the primary), with its publication date [Sn].

- ❌ "该模型是目前最强的开源模型。" (un-anchored, will be false in weeks)
- ✅ "截至 2026 年 6 月，它在 <benchmark> 上领先同期开源模型 [Sn]。" (dated, ages well)

**Why:** AI news goes stale in days and benchmarks are leapfrogged constantly. A dated,
primary-sourced claim survives; an un-anchored superlative becomes a visible error. The
audit's freshness WARN exists to force this conversation, not to be silenced.
